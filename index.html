Research Tools

Use the tools below as simpler shortcuts to access some of the public sources we lean on. More will be added over time, and if you have any suggesstions (or notice any bugs) please message Mike!

Business Layoff & Closure Listing

Explore all WARN logs published by DOL. Display data to ZCTA and city level with easy export. Data is updated monthly with excel and pdf export options.

Labor Force & Unemployment API

Pull the latest monthly labor force and unemployment to the county level. Automatically aggregates for multiple counties selected.

NLRB Unionization Attempts & Results

Explore the latest results of unionization efforts around the country. Data sourced from NLRB and can be searched by city and county. Updated monthly.

Weekly Unemployment Insurance Claims

Explore and pull weekly unemployment insurance claims. Data is available at county and 2-digit industry level. Updated monthly.

Workforce Assets

Find high schools, post-secondary schools, and community organizations dedicated to building workforce. Search for enrollment and completions by ONET Career Clusters.

Pandemic Assistance Data

Explore and pull loans and grants given to businesses for pandemic relief. Data is avilable at business and industry level, inluding attributes like race, gender, etc.

City Comparison Tool

Compare metro areas across a variety of attributes. Perform ad hoc analysis and export selections as a table. Datasets are typically updated annually, with some expections.

Migration Data

View migration patterns between states, metro areas, and counties over time. Pull charts and/or tables giving net migration by county, metro, or state in last 5 years.

County Index

Compiled data across four categories and aggregated into an index for identifying areas of strength and opportunity across Georgia's counties. Designed for Georgia Academy and county level leadership.
